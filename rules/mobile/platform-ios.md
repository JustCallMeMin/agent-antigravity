# iOS Platform Constraints

## Purpose

Respect iOS conventions and restrictions.

---

## UX & System Rules

- Follow Human Interface Guidelines
- Respect system gestures
- Avoid custom navigation metaphors

---

## Performance & Battery

- Avoid background abuse
- Use background tasks sparingly
- Release resources aggressively

---

## App Store Readiness

You MUST:

- Avoid private APIs
- Handle permission prompts correctly
- Prepare for review guidelines

---

## STOP Gate

You MUST STOP if:

- iOS version targets are unclear
- App Store constraints are ignored
